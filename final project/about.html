About Us

"EcoFeed" is a website which becomes an interface between donar, bio plant and farmer.The main objective behind making this website is to setup hustle free ecosystem for food waste donar to dispose waste and also for the bio-plant to get waste to create the bio gas and for farmer to get good quality fertilizer.

In our system waste would be collected from the house or resturant or any party plot willing to donate left over food(waste food) and would be send to the bio plant for further processing and the waste generated after making bio gas (namely slurry) would be collected from the plant and would be further send to farm as a fertilizer. Also our system will validate every farmers request for fertilizer
